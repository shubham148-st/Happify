Three-Minute Breathing Space {% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

A quick exercise for busy days to enhance mindfulness and reduce stress.

  1. Set a timer for three minutes.
  2. Sit comfortably and close your eyes if you wish. Notice your thoughts and feelings.
  3. Focus on your breath, feeling the air flow in and out.
  4. Expand your awareness to your whole body, noting sensations and posture.

Listening Mindfulness{% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

Engage deeply with the sounds around you to cultivate mindfulness.

  1. Close your eyes or softly gaze at a spot.
  2. Identify a sound in your environment.
  3. Listen deeply, noting its tone, rhythm, and volume.
  4. If your mind wanders, acknowledge it and return to the sound.

Dishwashing Mindfulness{% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

A calming technique to reduce anxiety and enhance body awareness.

  1. As you wash dishes, pay attention to the sensations: water temperature, sponge texture, and smells.
  2. If your mind drifts, gently bring it back to the task at hand.

Body Scan{% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

A calming technique to reduce anxiety and enhance body awareness.

  1. Close your eyes or maintain a soft gaze.
  2. Focus on each part of your body, starting from your feet to your head.
  3. Notice sensations without judgment, then return to your feet if desired.

Four-Seven-Eight Mindful Breathing{% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

A deep breathing exercise to alleviate anxiety and improve sleep.

  1. Close your eyes or softly gaze at a spot.
  2. Inhale for a count of four.
  3. Hold for a count of seven.
  4. Exhale for a count of eight, breathing deeply from your belly.

People-Watching{% bs_icon 'link-45deg' size='2rem' %}

Observe others to detach from your thoughts and practice awareness.

  1. Notice the people around you.
  2. Avoid forming judgments or stories about them.
  3. Optionally, engage with others through a smile or greeting.
